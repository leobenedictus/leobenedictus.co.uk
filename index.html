I write novels and articles. I spent 15 years as a freelance feature writer, mainly for the Guardian, and also wrote for the BBC, New Scientist, Prospect, and the London Review of Books. My journalism has won awards from Amnesty International, the Commission for Racial Equality, and the Work Foundation.

At the beginning of 2020, I joined Full Fact, where I write mainly about science and statistics. I am a self-taught data journalist and build simple tools using AI models.

I live in Brighton. My literary agent is Sophie Lambert at C+W. Feel free to email me at leobenedictus@substack.com.
